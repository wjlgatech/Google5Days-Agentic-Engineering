# 🧒 The 15-year-old Explorer — your path

**You want:** to build something real and cool today, and actually understand it.
**You don't need:** a degree, money, or any setup. Just curiosity and a terminal.

## Your first win (10 minutes)
Go to **[M1 · Your First Agent Loop → 15-explorer](../modules/M1-your-first-agent-loop/15-explorer.md)**.
Run a real agent, watch it think, add your own tool. Get the green ✓.

## Why this is worth your time
Everyone's talking about "AI agents." After M1 you'll have *built one* and be able to explain
it in a sentence — which is more than most adults can. That's your superpower: you learn by
*making*, not memorizing.

## Where you're headed (the map)
1. **M1 — build & run an agent loop** ← start here
2. M2 — give your agent more tools (make it *do* more)
3. M3 — give it memory (so it remembers you)
4. M4 — prove it works (testing = your trophy cabinet)
5. M5 — share it with the world

## Your habit
Every time you build something, make a tiny test that *proves* it works (our `--selftest`).
"It looks like it works" is a trap; "the check is green" is the truth. Carry that everywhere.
