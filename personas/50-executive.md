# 💼 The 50-year-old EVP / Executive — your path

**You want:** strategic clarity — where AI creates durable value, what to fund, and how to
govern it — in the least time.
**Your edge:** you fund operating capability that compounds, not demos that rot.

## Your first win (15 minutes)
**[M1 · Your First Agent Loop → 50-executive](../modules/M1-your-first-agent-loop/50-executive.md)** —
no jargon: what an agent is, where value/risk sit, and the three questions that separate real
capability from theater.

## The map (your lens: capital & control)
1. **M1 — strategic mental model** ← start here
2. M2/M3 — *why* proprietary tools + data are the moat (the model is rented)
3. **M4 — Quality** = your assurance that funded agents are trustworthy
4. **M5 — Production** = the last-mile cost (≈80%) you must budget for
5. The hub itself — a template for an AI-native operating model

## Your decisions to set as policy
- **No initiative funded without** a named business metric, a deterministic success gate, an
  owner, and a human-in-the-loop control.
- **Fund capability, not pilots** — tools + evaluation + governance + the feedback loop.
- **Insist on compounding:** every production failure becomes a permanent test within days.

## Your reusable asset
A one-page investment thesis per initiative (value + control made explicit) that Finance and
Risk would both sign. The [`memory/decisions/`](../memory/decisions/) log is organizational
memory compounding — the opposite of re-buying the same demo every year.
